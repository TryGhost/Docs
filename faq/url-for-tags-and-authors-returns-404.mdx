---
title: "URL For Tags And Authors Returns 404 Errors"
description: "The tag and author taxonomies must be present in routes.yaml otherwise the URLs will not exist. By default, Ghost installs with the following:"
---

***

```yaml
taxonomies:
  tag: /tag/{slug}/
  author: /author/{slug}/
```

The permalink for tag and author can be amended, but taxonomies for tag and author must always be present for the URL for individual tags and authors to function correctly.
